## Webinar: Improving Data Quality at the Source: A Data Quality Toolkit for Biocollections

This webinar was presented on 11 June 2026 as the first part of the GBIF North America 2026 Nodes Meeting and Training.
 
**Presenters:**
- **Katie Pearson**, Symbiota Support Hub Data Manager
- **Cat Chapman**, iDigBio & GBIF US Data Wrangler

**Topics covered:**
- Common data quality issues ad "dealbreakers" for ingestion into aggregators (e.g. GBIF)
- Available tools to improve data quality
